About the Game

Minecraft is a sandbox game where players explore, build, and survive in blocky worlds. With creative and survival modes, players can express creativity or take on challenges with friends.

Gameplay

Survival

Survival mode challenges players to gather resources, fight mobs, and stay alive. Discover biomes, craft tools, and face the Ender Dragon. How You Can Play

Play Minecraft on PC (Java Edition) or consoles/mobile (Bedrock Edition). Join friends using Realms for cross-platform multiplayer fun.
